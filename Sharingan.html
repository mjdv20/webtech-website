The Sharingan

The Sharingan is the dōjutsu kekkei genkai of the Uchiha clan that appears selectively among its members. It is regarded as one of the "Three Great Dōjutsu", the others being the Byakugan and the Rinnegan.

The Mangekyou Sharingan

The Mangekyou Sharingan is an advanced eye technique in the anime and manga series Naruto and Boruto that grants the user powerful abilities:

Awakening: A Sharingan user can awaken the Mangekyou Sharingan after experiencing a traumatic event, such as the death of a loved one. Abilities: The Mangekyou Sharingan grants the user two distinct abilities, one in each eye, such as the ability to cast genjutsu (illusionary techniques) or use unique techniques like Amaterasu and Tsukuyomi.

Susano'o: If the Mangekyou Sharingan is awakened in both eyes, the user can summon a Susano'o, one of the strongest powers in Naruto.

Chakra consumption: Wielding the Mangekyou Sharingan consumes a large amount of chakra.

Name: The name Mangekyou Sharingan translates to "Kaleidoscope Copy Wheel Eye".

Eternal Mangekyou Sharingan

The Eternal Mangekyo Sharingan is a powerful eye in the anime and manga series Naruto that grants the user many abilities, including:

Permanently restored vision: The user's vision is no longer harmed by using the eye.

No negative side effects: The user no longer experiences bodily harm or chakra drain.

All the powers of the original eyes: The user gains all the powers of their original eyes.

Stronger than the Mangekyo Sharingan: The Eternal Mangekyo Sharingan is much stronger than the Mangekyo Sharingan.
