What are the Uchihas?

The Uchiha clan is one of the four noble clans of Konohagakure that is reputed to be the village's strongest because of their Sharingan and natural battle prowess.


The Uchiha followed the lineage of Indra, the older son of the Six Paths Sage, who bestowed his descendants with his special eyes, the Sharingan, and his capacity for feeling deep, powerful emotions that teetered on the line between love and hatred.
